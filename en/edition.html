Background

The Melisso editions have produced a collection of tutoring books in collaboration with teachers who have transcribed their lessons into books. A company on a human scale with the desire to share its knowledge and its pedagogy.
Achieved goals : Create a main pattern, transcribed on all the books, which unites and gives the feeling of collection. A color code which aims to differentiate the school subjects.
