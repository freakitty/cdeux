Background

La Bonne Entente is a grocery store and a bar / restaurant in Pontoise seeking to improve its store but also its image. Highlight the quality products but also the convivial aspect of the place.
Achieved goals : An identity that can be adapted to the different places thanks to a color code. A festive and friendly communication.
